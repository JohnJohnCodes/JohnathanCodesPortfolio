A Little About Me

If this were 2018 I would describe myself as a former accountant with a knack for Excel, who loves peanut butter, protein shakes and the gym, who hit his stride as a data analyst & report developer in the tech industry. In late 2018 I said goodbye to the US & the American dream to travel the world. Everything was going great until March 2020 when COVID-19 effectively shut the world down.

During one of my quarantine boredom spells I found a couple of coding sites and started learning how to create wesbites. Soon I found myself staying up late at night to code. While, I don't claim to be a pro, I am pretty good and I would love the challenge of working on your website to further improve my skills.

It is my goal as a freelance front-end developer to be able to gain a name for myself by designing responsive websites that are both user-friendly and interactive so I am able to continue traveling around the globe once this pandemic is over.
